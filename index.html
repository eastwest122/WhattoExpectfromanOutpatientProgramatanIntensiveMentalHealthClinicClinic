What Is an Outpatient Program?

An outpatient program is a type of treatment plan for individuals who need assistance with substance abuse or mental health disorders, but do not require 24/7 medical supervision. These programs are ideal for individuals who have responsibilities such as work or family but still need structured therapy and support.
